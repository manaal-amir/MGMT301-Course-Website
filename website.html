Sustainability in Business Management

This course explores the fundamental principles of sustainable business practices and their integration into modern management strategies. We will examine how organizations can balance economic growth with environmental responsibility and social equity.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the triple bottom line framework (People, Planet, Profit)
  • Analyze case studies of successful sustainable business transformations
  • Develop strategies for implementing sustainability initiatives
  • Evaluate the business case for corporate social responsibility

Key Topics

  • Circular economy principles
  • ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) metrics
  • Sustainable supply chain management
  • Green marketing and consumer behavior
